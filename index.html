Vanguard Peroid







The Vanguard period lasted about 4 months, less than half the time of this migration period.

What is ExLibris' process????




Short deadlines with inadequate training and documentation


Tons of support after you turn in the forms


They will work with you, if needed one on one, to ensure your forms are correct


This process has seemed to work for all of the other colleges that have migrated to Alma, so it must work?

Lessons learned from the Vanguard period





1. Get your Alma Alma Administration Certification

2. You can make any changes set by the migration forms once you receive your Alma instance

3. Spend a good deal of your time reviewing your data

4. Once the production instance is delivered all configurations and changes will be kept for when you go live
